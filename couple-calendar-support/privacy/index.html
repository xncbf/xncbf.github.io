Privacy Policy

Couple Calendar / 커플 캘린더 · Last updated: 2026-04-08

Couple Calendar helps couples manage anniversaries, events, and widgets together. This policy explains what information we use and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Sign in with Apple account identifier and email information provided by Apple
  • Events, anniversaries, and customization data you create in the app
  • Pairing information needed to connect two users as a couple
  • Diagnostic information required to operate core services and fix issues

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate your account
  • Sync events and anniversaries between paired users
  • Provide widgets, reminders, and app personalization
  • Maintain app reliability and security

3. Data Storage

Couple Calendar uses Firebase services to store and sync app data. Data is used only to provide the service and is not sold to third parties.

4. Sharing

Your data is shared only with your explicitly paired partner inside the app, and only for the purpose of using Couple Calendar features.

5. Your Choices

  • You can delete events and anniversaries inside the app at any time
  • You can delete your account from the app settings
  • You can manage subscriptions from your App Store account settings
